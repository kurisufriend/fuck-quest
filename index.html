FUCK QUEST:
THE UNOFFICIAL ARCHIVE
>what is this?
this website is a collected archive of Fuck Quest, a collaborative work of smut fiction that ran on 4chan from 2014 to 2022. There exists already an official directory for FQ and other works by its author, OP Studios, at op-studios.net, but my goal is to create a more permanent self-hosted version, with fixed images, homogenous threads, and easy navigation.
>navigation
All FQ threads have been saved in their original form, and are represented in imageboard format.
Each thread has a link to the next in chronological order, and each "marked" post (i.e. story-related OP post) has a link to the next. If desired, enabling "reader mode" will make all non-story/non-OP posts invisible.
THE CHART has also been re-imagined (with the same information) for your viewing pleaseure.
